About Me

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Indranil Gupta. My research interests are primarily in Systems for Machine Learning, Distributed Systems, and Cloud Computing. My recent research focuses on designing distributed ML systems in constrained environments including device-level, cluster-level, data-level, and paradigm-level constraints. I have worked with IBM Research, Nokia Bell Labs, and Google. I obtained a B.S. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University.

I am currently on the job market, looking for full-time positions in industry. Here is my CV. Please feel free to contact me via bj2@illinois.edu.
